Health Care Facility Features at System Clinic
Modern Equipment, Comfortable and Safe Environment, Diverse Care Services
Integrated Medical Team, Rapid Emergency Response

“Healthcare facility” refers to a place that specializes in providing medical and therapeutic services to patients. At System Clinic, we view our health care facility as a comprehensive place that combines advanced medical technology and a team of dedicated experts to provide the highest level of healthcare.

Neurosurgery

This service includes surgeries related to the brain, spinal cord and peripheral nerves. We diagnose and treat conditions such as brain tumors, herniated discs and nerve injuries.

Read More

Neurology

Focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders such as stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, Alzheimer's and Parkinson's diseases.

Read More

Radiology

It involves the use of imaging techniques such as X-rays, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), and computed tomography (CT) to diagnose medical problems and monitor changes in patients' condition.

Read More

Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation

Focuses on promoting healing and improving physical function through rehabilitation and physical therapy programs. Treatment includes spinal cord injuries, spine injuries, and chronic conditions.

Read More

Medical Consultations

We provide specialized consultations to provide medical advice, diagnosis, and treatment planning for various conditions based on the patient's symptoms and medical history.

Read More

Pain Management

It includes strategies for managing chronic and acute pain, including drug therapies, physical therapy, and interventional techniques such as injections.

Read More

Neurodiagnostic Testing

It involves performing tests such as electroencephalography (EEG) and electromyography (EMG) to diagnose neurological disorders.

Read More

Preventive Medicine

Focuses on disease prevention and public health promotion through regular checkups, and providing advice and guidance to maintain a healthy lifestyle. This includes early detection tests, health awareness programs, and vaccinations.

Read More

Genetic Counseling

This service provides counseling about genetic diseases and genetic risks. This includes helping people understand genetic testing, advising on treatment options, and helping families make informed decisions about genetic health.
