FAQ

Here are some instructions and frequently asked questions about home services, clinic services, System Clinic.

What services does the home service at System Clinic include?

System Clinic's home service includes medical consultations, routine check-ups, emergency treatment, and medical delivery of products and medications to your home. We are committed to providing excellent medical care without the need to leave your home.

What are the available times for home service?

We provide home service around the clock, including weekends and holidays. You can choose the time that suits you by coordinating with our team when placing the order.

What clinic services does System Clinic provide?

We offer a wide range of clinic services including comprehensive medical examinations, specialized consultations, emergency treatment, and various medical procedures. We strive to provide comprehensive medical care according to the highest standards.

How can I get the results of the tests I had done at the clinic?

You can get your test results by visiting the clinic or by calling us. You can also check your results through your personal account on our website if this service is available.

What benefits does System Clinic offer?

System Clinic provides a comprehensive range of medical services including home care, outpatient services, and medical delivery. We are committed to providing excellent and convenient healthcare to the highest standards.

What payment methods are available for System Clinic service prices?

We offer a variety of payment methods including cash, credit cards, and online payment. You can choose the method that suits you when placing your order or when visiting the clinic.
